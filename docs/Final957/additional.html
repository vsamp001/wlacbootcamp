About

This website was made by Victor Samperio to add more information to a research paper. This research paper and now website was for my Media Technologies, Power and Knowledge: A Historical Approach seminar class while I was working on my History degree at the University of California Riverside. The general topic is to examine superhero adaptations both from comics to other media and from other media into comics – and see which things remain true to the source material and what is changed. My research led to choose only one superhero Superman because of the extensive history of the character.
