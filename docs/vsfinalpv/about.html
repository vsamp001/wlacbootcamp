About this site.

The purpose of this site is for me to write down my thoughts on some tv shows I watch. Since the way people watch television has changed it is very hard to talk to other people without them saying "SPOILERS! I haven't watched it yet." So if I have something to say I can write it down instead of trying to see if my friends are caught up on tv shows.

The purpose of the homepage is to introduce the 3 main shows that I talked about. The Arrow page has a synopsis and trailer of the show and has an episode guide that takes you to my thoughts on the episode, Flash and Supergirl page is the same. The TV page has the tv shows that im watching right now, there are so many other shows that are not listed because they are on a break right now.

Most of the pictures use fancybox and the headers are also slideshow. The Tv page has the table element, and the about page has the form to subscribe to a newsletter.

The most challenging aspect of the site is the changes from mobile to tablet to desktop and having the correct styles. Also the amount of work to write about 3 shows.

The main Arrow, Flash and Supergirl pages is what im most pleased about in the site. How each has an individual background and color scheme.
